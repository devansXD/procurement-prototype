About Compass

Compass is the NHS digital platform for value-based procurement of medical technologies. We centralize trusted sources of evidence to help procurement professionals and clinicians make faster, more consistent, and evidence-based decisions.

Data Sources

  • NICE Medtech Guidance
  • ODEP Accreditation
  • DTAC Passporting
  • NHS Trust Evaluations
  • MHRA Registration Data

Compass centralizes trusted sources of evidence but does not validate evidence. It only imposes minimum standards for displayed information.
