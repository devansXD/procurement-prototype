What you can do with Compass

  • Search and compare medical technologies using clinical outcomes and value-based criteria
  • Access evaluations and reviews from other NHS trusts
  • Submit your own product evaluations to share with the NHS community
  • Upload documents and share clinical data with peer trusts
  • Connect with other procurement and clinical teams for peer discussion
  • Make evidence-based procurement decisions using standardized product data

Alpha prototype

Compass is currently in alpha testing. Your feedback will help us improve the service for all NHS organizations.

What does alpha mean?

Alpha is an early version of the service. We're testing our assumptions and learning from users like you. Some features may change based on feedback.

What happens next

  1. We'll verify your NHS email address
  2. Your account will be reviewed (usually within 1 working day)
  3. You'll receive confirmation and can start using Compass
  4. You can invite colleagues from your trust to join

Data security

All data shared on Compass is:

  • Stored on secure NHS infrastructure
  • Encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Only accessible to verified NHS staff
  • Subject to NHS data governance standards
