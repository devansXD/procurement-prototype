Excluded Quick review District hospital

Evaluation type
2-week desk review and supplier meeting
Evaluation period
May 2023
Department
Procurement, Medical Devices

Why excluded

Trust decided not to proceed because they were already mid-way through a large-scale infusion pump replacement program with a different supplier. The existing contract had 3 years remaining and standardization across all wards was a priority. Product itself was well-regarded but timing didn't align with procurement cycle.

Feedback provided

  • Product specifications met requirements
  • Pricing was competitive
  • Decision was driven by contract timing, not product concerns
  • Would consider for next procurement cycle (2026)
